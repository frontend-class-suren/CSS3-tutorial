About Me

Hi I am Monkey D. Luffy ,The Frontend Developer & The main protagonist in anime 'One piece'.


Growing up in a lively coastal town, I, Luffy, shared dreams with my pals and revered the adventurous tales of pirates. My world turned upside down when I consumed the mystical Devil Fruit, granting me rubber powers but leaving me unable to swim. A pivotal moment occurred when my daring nature attracted danger, leading my mentor Shanks to lose an arm protecting me. That incident ignited an unyielding resolve within me to become the Pirate King. With a carefree spirit and an unwavering loyalty to my friends, I set sail on a daring journey to find the fabled One Piece and carve my name into the annals of pirate legend.
